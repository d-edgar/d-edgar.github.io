About Me

Hi, I'm David Edgar, an experienced IT professional, app developer, and cybersecurity enthusiast. I currently manage technical support services at Christopher Newport University, where I lead a team focused on IT infrastructure, security, and endpoint management. Passionate about learning and innovation, I continuously explore new technologies and best practices to improve efficiency and security in IT operations.

When I'm not working, I enjoy running, spending time outdoors, and being a dad to two wonderful daughters. I'm always open to collaboration and discussions on IT, cybersecurity, and software development.
